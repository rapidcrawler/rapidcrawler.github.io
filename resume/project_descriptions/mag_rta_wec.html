Let's assume the following values for the daily consumption required to run the Dubai Metro:

  • Daily electricity consumption: 80,835 kWh
  • Daily water consumption: 51,271 gallons

We will calculate monthly consumption and apply the tariff structure for electricity and water.

Step 1: Calculate Monthly Electricity Consumption

Daily electricity consumption: 80,835 kWh

Monthly electricity consumption: 80,835 kWh/day × 30 days = 2,425,052 kWh/month

Now, apply the slab tariff:

  • For the first 10,000 kWh: 23 fils/kWh
  • For consumption above 10,000 kWh: 38 fils/kWh
  1. Cost for the first 10,000 kWh:

    10,000 × 23 = 230,000 fils = 230 AED

  2. Cost for the remaining 2,415,052 kWh:

    2,415,052 × 38 = 91,682,000 fils = 91,682 AED

Total Monthly Electricity Cost: 230 + 91,682 = 91,912 AED

Step 2: Calculate Monthly Water Consumption

Daily water consumption: 51,271 gallons

Monthly water consumption: 51,271 gallons/day × 30 days = 1,538,125 gallons/month

Now, apply the water consumption tariff:

  • For the first 10,000 gallons: 0.03 per gallon
  • For the next 10,000 gallons (10,001 to 20,000): 0.035 per gallon
  • For consumption above 20,000 gallons: 0.04 per gallon
  1. Cost for the first 10,000 gallons:

    10,000 × 0.03 = 300 AED

  2. Cost for the next 10,000 gallons (10,001 to 20,000):

    10,000 × 0.035 = 350 AED

  3. Cost for the remaining 1,518,125 gallons:

    1,518,125 × 0.04 = 60,725 AED

Total Monthly Water Cost: 300 + 350 + 60,725 = 61,375 AED

Step 3: Final Cost Summary

  • Monthly Electricity Cost: 91,912 AED
  • Monthly Water Cost: 61,375 AED

Total Monthly Cost (Electricity + Water)

91,912 + 61,375 = 153,287 AED

Total Annual Cost (Electricity + Water)

153,287 AED * 12 months = 1,838,240 AED (approximately $500,000)
